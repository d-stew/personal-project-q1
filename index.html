Agree: People have a fundamental right to health care, and the government should provide coverage or subsidize health-insurance for everybody.


Disagree: There is no such thing as global warming -- it's all natural climatic variation. We should not spend money trying to fix a problem that probably won't even affect us.

Prioritize climate change/clean energy

Agree: Climate change and global warming are serious problems caused by humans. We should end our dependence on fossil fuels and pursue sustainable energy alternatives.


Disagree: Drug use is immoral and drugs are a poison to our youth and our society. We can win with enough resources -- more police, more border patrols and harsher prison sentences.

End the War on Drugs

Agree: Drug abuse is a public health issue, not a criminal justice issue. Harsh drug laws disproportionately affect minorities and should be repealed. Marijuana should be decriminalized or legalized.


Disagree: Higher taxes hurt the economy, slow job creation and discourage people from starting or expanding their own businesses. Tax cuts for the wealthy actually end up helping those with lower incomes.

Raise taxes on corporations and the wealthy

Agree: The top 1% should not have more wealth than the bottom 99%. Progressive taxation is a cornerstone of society and the rich should pay a higher share of taxes. Tax loopholes that only benefit corporations must be closed.


Disagree: Illegal immigrants are criminals, and they should not be allowed in our country. We need to increase border security, deport illegal immigrants already here and remove all their social benefits.

Pathway to citizenship for illegal aliens

Agree: Illegal aliens that prove themselves as productive members of society should be given citizenship. Immigration increases our diversity and ultimately helps our economy.


Disagree: Social Security should remain under the control of the government to ensure all Americans have a secure retirement. Funds should not be exposed to risky investments.

Privatize Social Security

Agree: The government should not be in charge of our retirement funds. Social Security should be run like Roth IRAs or other private pension plans, and individuals should have some control over their investments.


Disagree: Our military is already bigger than the rest of the world combined. That money could be much better spent on social programs or other domestic issues. An overly-large military just creates enemies and destroys our economy.

Expand the military

Agree: As a world leader, we have an obligation to maintain a strong military, especially as certain regions become even more unstable. More spending is also necessary to ensure our national security.


Disagree: It is our right and duty to take military action abroad when our national interests are threatened. We also have a responsibility to protect our allies by any means necessary.

Avoid foreign entanglements

Agree: The United States is not the world police. Wars are expensive and cost lives, and almost always cause more harm than good.

Support the Trans-Pacific Partnership

Agree: The TPP boosts economic growth by removing tariffs on U.S. exports, with $77 billion of extra income going to U.S. workers. The agreement also cuts down on wildlife trafficking and prevents environmental abuses.


Disagree: Marriage is between a man and a woman. Allowing gays to marry would hurt the institution of marriage and impose an immoral lifestyle on children.

Support same-sex marriage

Agree: Gays have a constitutional right to Equal Protection under the law and should have the same rights and status as everyone else. The government has no right to discriminate based on sexual orientation.


Disagree: Separation of church and state is essential. The government should not provide tax money to religious organizations, promote religion in public places or allow public school prayers.

Keep God in the public sphere

Agree: America was founded on belief in God and Western law is based heavily off the Ten Commandments. We need religious values in our society, in our schools, and in our government.


Disagree: Human needs come first, and Earth is our domain to do with as we see fit. Strict regulations hurt businesses and enforcing them is a waste of money.

Stricter environmental regulations

Agree: We have to balance economic needs with environmental needs. Nature must be respected and protected, even if it places a burden on businesses and individuals.


Disagree: Voter fraud undermines the process of democracy and we need to fight it. ID requirements for voting are perfectly reasonable.

Make voter registration easier

Agree: We should make it easier to vote, not harder. Voter ID laws are mostly intended to suppress minority voting and don't actually stop voter fraud.


Disagree: If you have nothing to hide, you have nothing to worry about. NSA surveillance helps us fight terrorism by identifying threats before they become a problem.

End NSA surveillance/data collection

Agree: Government surveillance is a violation of our constitutional rights. We should not give up our right to privacy in the name of national security.


Disagree: Raising the minimum wage will make prices go up and jobs disappear, as many companies would have to offset increased labor costs. It would also reduce the incentive to pursue more education and move up to a higher-paying job.

Increase the minimum wage

Agree: Every citizens deserves to make a living wage. $7.25 an hour -- only $15,080 a year -- is unnaceptable for someone working full-time. Higher wages mean more spending, which helps our economy in the long run.


Disagree: Campaign donations are free speech, and should not be limited for corporations nor for individuals. As long as Super-PACs do not coordinate with campaigns themselves, they should be free to spend money as they see fit.

Overturn Citizens United

Agree: Private funding of campaigns undermines democracy, as those with more money have more influence over the political process. Campaigns should be publicly funded to reduce the power of lobbyists and special interest groups.


Disagree: Abortion is immoral because it kills a human being, and should not be tolerated. A fetus has rights of its own -- including the right to life -- that cannot be violated.

Abortion is a woman's unrestricted right

Agree: Abortion is a private decision between a woman and her doctor. The government has no business telling a woman what to do with her body. Women should have access to safe, legal abortions if they want to make that decision.


Disagree: The wage gap is not sexism. Legally requiring equal pay just creates more government regulations and opens the door for lawsuits.

Require equal pay for equal work

Agree: Women's median annual earnings are 77.5% of men's earnings. This is institutional sexism and we should require employers to pay men and women the same salary for the same job.


Disagree: Legally requiring the hiring of minorities or women is just reverse discrimination. Quotas based on race or gender are wrong, regardless of who they benefit. The best person for the job should be hired, period.

Support Affirmative Action programs

Agree: We need to make up for past injustice. Until women and minorities are proportionately represented in the upper classes of society and the workplace, the government should give them a hand up.
